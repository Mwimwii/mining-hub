Our Services

Geological Consulting Services

CGL provides tailor-made consulting services across the full geological spectrum.

  • Arrangements for exploration and Mining permits
  • Exploration sampling, Analysis and QA/QC
  • Property Appraisals and Evaluations
  • Mineral exploration field services
  • Field Structural Analysis
  • Field Mapping
  • Resource Estimations

Environmental Consulting Services

CGL provides consultancy services related to compliance, governance systems, environmental and social assessment, and more.

  • Hydropower schemes and electrification
